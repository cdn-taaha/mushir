Know Your Doctor

Dr. MUSHIR M. SIDDIQUI

Consultant Orthopaedic Surgeon

Dr. Mushir M. Siddiqui is a Consultant Orthopaedic Surgeon practising in Mumbai.

Specialized expertise in Joint Replacement, Trauma, Spine, Paediatric, Sports Injury and Arthroscopic Surgery.

He is a Post Graduate "Gold Medalist" in Field of Orthopedics with "Fellowship in Joint Replacement".

He practises in Andheri and Jogeshwari areas of Mumbai. He visits many of the Hospitals for Emergency, in-patient and Surgery visits.

He has demonstrated positive results and patient satisfaction and believes in promoting maximum health and mobility from Geriatric to Paediatric age group.

Dr. Mushir believes in treating every patient with utmost care and goes an extra mile to make his patient comfortable.

Areas of Expertise

Joint Replacement

Advanced hip, knee, and shoulder replacement surgeries using cutting-edge techniques for optimal outcomes and faster recovery.

Trauma & Fracture Care

Expert treatment of all types of fractures and traumatic injuries with state-of-the-art fixation methods and rehabilitation protocols.

Sports Injury

Specialized care for sports-related injuries including ligament tears, cartilage damage, and musculoskeletal trauma.

Arthroscopic Surgery

Minimally invasive arthroscopic procedures for joints, offering faster recovery, less pain, and minimal scarring.

Spine & Back Surgery
